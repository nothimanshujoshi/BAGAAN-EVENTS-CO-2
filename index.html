Our Philosophy

Crafting Organic Celebrations

At BAGAAN, we move away from cookie-cutter designs to bring you spaces that breathe. Inspired by nature, terracotta textures, and warm sunsets, our styling is for those who appreciate the poetry in details.

Based in the artistic heart of Hauz Khas, Delhi, we specialize in intimate weddings, earthy floral installations, and setups that feel incredibly personal and soulful.
